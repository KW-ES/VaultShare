# Market Risk

**What is Risk?**
- How prices in market impact out PnL
- Element of uncertainty
	- Possibility of loss that we will lose money
- When talk about risk, it's more about what will we lose


>![[IMG-20260316090135993.png]]

<br>

- **Risk Attributes:**
	- Uncertainty
	- Possibility of Loss
- **Risk Management (4 key pillars)**
	- 1. Avoidance
		- Core function:
		- How manage your markets, products, sectors
	- 2. Reduction
		- Exposure?
	- 3. Sharing
	- 4. Retention
		- We know we can manage it and manage it effectively
- **Risk Vs return**
	- Higher Risk → Higher Return

> [!warning]- A loss known with certainty ≠ risk
